Pancake Recipe

Above is an image of a classical pan cake

My grandma taught me how to make them to be fluffy, it's easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • salt
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Sift the dry ingredients
  2. Make a well, then add wet ingredients. Stir to combine.
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle or pan
  4. Cook for 2 -3 minutes, then flip
  5. Continue cooking untill brown on both sides
